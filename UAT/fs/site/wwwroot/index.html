Your Pets Covered

You can’t predict when your pet is going to get sick or injured, and the unexpected costs associated with vet bills can really hurt your wallet- that’s where we come in. Just as health insurance offsets medical costs for your 2-legged family members, 4Paws Insurance helps offset those costs for your 4-legged family members. Our flexible plans allow you to customize your policy and choose the coverage that fits your pet and your budget.

Why You Should Choose Us to Protect Your Pet

Keep Your Vet
You can continue to see your current vet, or choose to take your pet to any licensed veterinarian in the U.S., its territories and Canada.

Low Annual Deductibles
Do you want to pay a deductible for every new accident or illness? We don’t, so you can choose from 4 annual deductibles that only have to be met once per policy period.

Exam Fees Included
We cover the vet exam fee related to a covered illness or injury. Other pet insurance providers exclude this or require you to pay more for coverage.

Up to 90% Reimbursement
You can select your reimbursement level up to 90% for eligible services. We reimburse you based on the actual vet invoice that you submit.
